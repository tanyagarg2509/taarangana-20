LILAC DREAMS

Rules:
  • A team must have a minimum of 8 members. The maximum permissible limit for members is 15 per team + one backstage helper.
  • Time limit: 10 minutes including stage setup time. Points will be deducted for exceeding the time limit.
  • Theme selection is open.
  • Teams will be selected on the basis of their video auditions.
  • Contestants must carry their own costumes, makeup, props and other materials used.
  • Participants will be judged on theme, apparel, coordination, theme portrayal, X factor. Emphasis will be given to clothing, portrayal and originality.
  • Only one backstage helper is allowed.
  • Props are allowed. Lighting of candles, matches or cigarettes on stage is not allowed and will result in disqualification of the team.
  • The teams are responsible for their own music and sequence.
  • Music is to be submitted in CD/ pen drive. The teams are expected to label the CD or Pen drive properly to avoid confusion . They must submit them to the organizers at least an hour before the event starts. The team should carry at least two extra pen drives for backup.
  • The teams are required to clear the stage with the help of organizers immediately after their performance.
  • Vulgarity of any kind will lead to disqualification
  • A green room will be provided for changing purpose.

POP ART

Rules:
  • The theme for this event will be provided on the spot.
  • There can be a maximum of THREE participants in a team.
  • Time provided to all the participants to complete the task will be two hours ONLY.
  • Use of any external resources ( for example : internet, notes, books ) for inspiration is strictly prohibited.
  • PLAGIARISM of any form will not be accepted. It may result in elimination of the submitted entry.
  • Participants are requested to bring their own material and colours to enhance their artwork.
  • The verdict passed by the Judges shall be final and binding.

SYNNOVE

Rules:
  • Only solos allowed.
  • There will be 2 rounds in the event.
  • FIRST ROUND: Participants may choose whichever song / forte they wish to. Music has to be brought in a CD/ Pen Drive.
  • Time limit : 2 minutes
  • SECOND ROUND: This round will be a face off round conducted between participants who are shortlisted from the first round. Each participant will dance for a minute in the face off.
  • The final result will be declared on the basis of scores secured in the face off.
  • No costumes and props will be provided.
  • Order of performance will be decided on first come first serve basis.
  • Participants have to ensure that one copy of their edited song is with the event coordinator and one copy, as a backup, is present with them.
  • It is the contestant's duty to be careful and aware of the order of performance.

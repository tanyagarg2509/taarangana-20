SLAM POETRY

Rules:
  • The Poetry should be in English/Hindi.
  • This is a solo event ( single person as a participant only ) and, NO team efforts are allowed.
  • PLAGIARISM of any form will not be accepted. It may result in elimination of the submitted entry.
  • Use of any external resources ( for example : internet, notes, books ) for inspiration is strictly prohibited.
  • The poem at the time of registration should be different from that of the one in competition. That is no repetition is allowed.
  • Questioning after the poem recitation is allowed by the judge only, so be prepared.
  • The maximum time limit is 5 mins.
  • The verdict passed by the Judges shall be final and binding.

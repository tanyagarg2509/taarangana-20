MR & MISS TAARANGANA

Rules:
  • No costumes or props will be provided.
  • For the first round, participants will be asked to walk the ramp.
  • For the second round, the participants will introduce themselves in not more than two-three lines. This introduction will then be followed by talent show.
  • Any sound track used for this round must be brought in a CD/ Pen Drive.
  • Marks will be deducted in case of a time limit violation.
  • The third round will be an audience interaction round .
  • Fourth round will be a question /answer round.
  • Vulgarity in any form will not be allowed.
  • It is the contestant’s duty to be careful and aware of the order of performance.
  • Order of performance will be decided on first come first basis.

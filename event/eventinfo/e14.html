PITCH PERFECT

Rules:
  • Each team must consist of a minimum of 5 members. There can be, at most, 23 members in a team.
  • One vocalist is compulsory in every team.
  • Teams are not allowed to use any instruments.
  • Organizing committee shall not provide any accompanists.
  • ROUND : Teams are required to perform any song(s) of their choice. Songs may be in any language.
  • Time Limit - 8-10 minutes (including setup and clearance)
  • During the registration for the event, the teams will be required to send their entries to -
  • Participants will be judged on quality of vocals, rhythm, harmonies, tightness, synchronisation, overall impact(stage presence).
  • The verdict passed by the Judges shall be final and binding.

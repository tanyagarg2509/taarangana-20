RANGMANCH

Rules:
  • Team Size: Solo + 2SAs (for light and sound if required)
  • There will be 2 rounds in the event.
  • FIRST ROUND: Participants will have to prepare a performance for this round.
  • Time Limit : 2-3 minutes
  • SECOND ROUND: Participants qualifying for the final round will be given a situation to perform on.
  • Time Limit: 1-2 minutes
  • A preparation time of 10 minutes will be given.
  • Use of light-weighted Prop is allowed. Any kind of fluid, live animals, flame, heavy/sharp object or any material which has the possibility of damaging the stage is not allowed.
  • Miming or ventriloquism is STRICTLY not allowed.The act should be in Hindi or English or both. However short sub passages in other regional languages are allowed.
  • Production materials provided: 1 chair and 1 table. Any other production material has to be brought by the participants themselves, but the organisers hold the discretion of allowing it on stage.
  • Participants can have live music which should be brought in a pen drive in .mp3 format only.
  • Participants will be judged on Voice modulation, Variety of characters, Flow of expression, Acting skills, Overall impression.
  • The verdict passed by the Judges shall be final and binding.

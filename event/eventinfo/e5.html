KNIGHTS OF THE FALL

Prelims : The bands will be short listed on the basis of a preliminary round for which the teams are to send in a video recording of their performance.

KNIGHTS OF THE FALL

Rules:
  • Each band will be given 15 min (including sound check) to perform. The competing bands should perform at least one Original Composition in the stipulated time limit.
  • Performance slots will be allotted on first come first serve basis on the day of event.
  • The organisers shall provide lead guitar amplifier, bass guitar amplifier, standard 5 piece drum kit(single pedal base),corded mics, any equipments required apart from these shall be arranged for by the competing band itself.

REGISTER

General Guidelines:

  • ONLY ONLINE REGISTRATION, NO ON THE SPOT REGISTRATION.
  • THE TEAMS WILL BE SELECTED ON THE BASIS OF THE SUBMISSION OF THEIR PERFORMANCE, TO PERFORM ON THE ACTUAL DAY OF THE FEST
  • CONFIRMATION WILL BE SENT THROUGH MAIL
  • Only ONE ENTRY is allowed PER PARTICIPANT/TEAM.
  • FIRST COME, FIRST SERVE criteria of slotting.

      • Eligibility :

      Students enrolled in a valid course, at any college in possession of a valid identity card of the same institution may participate in the event.
